PRIVACY POLICY

I. COMMITMENT TO DATA PROTECTION

At TopHealthTrending, we recognize that your privacy is of paramount importance. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we utilize, store, and protect that data, and the rights you hold regarding your personal metrics. By interacting with TopHealthTrending.com (the "Site"), you consent to the data practices described in this comprehensive policy.

This document is designed to comply with international data protection standards, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), ensuring transparency and security for our global customer base.

II. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

In our pursuit of providing a premium shopping experience, we collect information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked to you. This is categorized into two main streams:

1. Information Provided Directly by You

When you make a purchase or attempt to make a purchase through the Site, we collect specific details including your name, billing address, shipping address, payment information (including credit card numbers, PayPal details, or other gateway identifiers), email address, and phone number. We refer to this as “Order Information.”

2. Information Collected Automatically

As you navigate TopHealthTrending, we automatically harvest certain data about your device, including details about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your hardware. Additionally, we track individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site. We refer to this as “Device Information.”

III. DATA COLLECTION TECHNOLOGIES (COOKIES & TRACKING)

We utilize various industry-standard technologies to collect Device Information:

  • Cookies: Small data files placed on your device or computer. For more information about cookies, and how to disable them, visit allaboutcookies.org.
  • Log Files: Track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • Web Beacons, Tags, and Pixels: Electronic files used to record information about how you browse the Site, often used for retargeting and marketing analytics.

IV. UTILIZATION OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

TopHealthTrending uses the Order Information that we collect generally to fulfill any orders placed through the Site (including processing your payment information, arranging for shipping, and providing you with invoices and/or order confirmations). Additionally, we use this Order Information to:

  • Communicate with you regarding your transaction or account status;
  • Screen our orders for potential risk or fraud;
  • When in line with the preferences you have shared with us, provide you with information or advertising relating to our products or services;
  • Optimize our product offerings and website performance through internal analytics.

We use the Device Information to help us screen for potential risk and fraud (in particular, your IP address), and more generally to improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with the Site, and to assess the success of our marketing and advertising campaigns).
